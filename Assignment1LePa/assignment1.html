Welcome to the Private School's management system.


From this site you will be able to handle all the data that a private school has. You will be able to ADD , EDIT or UPDATE the existing lists of the school. All the lists that you can handle from here are the followings : Students , Trainers , Courses , Assignments , Students Per Course , Trainers per Course , Assignments Per Course and Assignments per Student per Course.

This School Management System - Student Information System (SIS) is the core component of our comprehensive school management software solution for private schools. It provides a safe and protected place for our users to manage student data and share academic records securely within the professors and the managers.
